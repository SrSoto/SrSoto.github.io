# Transaction Monitoring Team Management: Agile 

My Head of Core Application promotion started when decided to challenge our product of Transaction Monitoring by starting the coordination of a team that grew up with the passing of months and quarters. A key for both the team and product success has been our transition to Agile methodologies.

* Starting Point: Restarted product with a little team to manage for achieving a six months MVP and a one year 1.0.0 version.
* Ending Point: Multiple current clients with instances deployed on 1.3.0 version and continuing on 2026; leading a team of 15 developers.
* Results: Multiple million $ contract signed for a successful full product dynamically developed in less than a year, commanding a team that is rapidly growing while being ordered using Agile methods.
